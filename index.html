About Me

Recent transplant to SF from Bend, OR. App Academy grad, recovering service industry worker, stage manager. Philosophy school drop-out. I have yet to pinpoint the meaning of life, but I've certainly pinned many hems, and I think I've found a common thread to tie it all together...

A colleague once passed on the advice "Keep calm and carry gaff tape" and that has stuck with me ever since. I love a good puzzle, and thrive most when working on a project or finding a creative solution to a problem (as we said in my a/A cohort, D.T.D. or Down To Debug!). I love to organize and am an admitted checklist-aholic.

Whether it has been working in local theater, managing a neighborhood grocery store, or cooking elaborate multi-course meals for friends on a week night, one thing always seems to be true- there's nothing I would rather do than to contribute to and be surrounded by my community.

That passion, and some fierce service industry burn-out post-pandemic led me to pursue a new career path. I started to teach myself coding, and discovered the exact efficacy and empowerment I was looking for. And I soon after dropped everything to join App Academy's software engineering bootcamp.

Send me a message and ask what I'm working on now, or check out my resume to learn more.

Projects

DreamyNote

Live Site

DreamyNote is a dream journaling web application modeled after Evernote, using a React frontend and Flask/Python backend. Take control of your dreams and unlock their hidden meanings with DreamyNote. Say goodbye to forgotten dreams and hello to a deeper understanding of yourself. This app makes it easy to document and analyze your dreams, so you can gain insights into your subconscious mind. Sign up to start your dream journey today!

Not OK, Cupid

Live Site

Not OK, Cupid is a clone of the popular dating site okCupid, using a React frontend and Flask/Python backend. It was a group project completed with three other developers at App Academy. It uses customizable preferences and quirky personality questions to "mismatch" users with each other in an effort to promote friendly communication between people who may not have otherwise talked.

HangOut

Live Site

HangOut is a clone of Meetup, our first full-stack project at App Academy, asing a React frontend and Expressjs backend. Users can join groups, attend events, or start their own group.
